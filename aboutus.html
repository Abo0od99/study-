The Problem

Jawabmed was started out frustration with the current education system in the Arab world . The average medical student in the Arab world spends a significant amount of time sourcing information to use for studying and revising. This usually comes in the form of dense textbooks or scattered lecture notes and slides .

Our Vision

Jawabmed will be the ' go to ' platform for online university education in the MENA region . Starting with medical sciences .

Our Missiond

Jawabmed is an online education tool for medical students and doctors in training in the Arab world . It covers the entire syllabus of medical school all in one easy to use , smart platform

Our Purpose

Jawabmed is an EdTech company based in Zarqa , Jordan . The main service we provide is an online education tool ( Question bank ) for medical students and doctors in training . The tool consists of a comprehensive question bank that covers the entire syllabus of medical school and the main medical exams that a typical medical student or doctor in training would encounter . The tool is built around modern studying techniques that are more efficient than traditional methods of studying . It also allows the user to track their progress along with real - time performance feedback.Jawabmed website is owned by Jawab Education Co ( شركة الجواب لدعم التعليم ) in Jordan .
